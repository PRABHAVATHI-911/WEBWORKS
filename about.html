ABOUT US

Dr.Rakshita Jain BAMS MD(Ayu)


Profile

Dr. Rakshitha Jain S.B. is a highly accomplished Ayurvedic practitioner with a strong academic background and extensive professional experience. Born in Saligrama, Mysuru, she completed her primary education in Saligrama and secondary education in Dakshina Kannada. She pursued her Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS) from SDM College of Ayurveda and Hospital, Udupi, and later earned her MD in Basic Principles of Ayurveda from SDM College of Ayurveda and Hospital, Hassan.

Professional Experience

  • Currently practicing Ayurveda at Aarohi Ayurveda, Dattagalli, Mysuru.
  • Srimatha Hospital, Kundapura.
  • Worked at SDM Hospital, Ujire.
  • Served as a Medical Officer for three years at the Government Taluk Hospital, H D Kote.

Achievements

  • Secured the 3rd rank in MD Ayurveda at Rajiv Gandhi University, Bengaluru.
  • Published over 7 articles in National and International Journals.
  • Presented 11 papers at National and International seminars.
  • Won 5 Best Paper Presenter awards at National and International seminars.
  • Actively contributed as a COVID warrior at Hassan Institute of Medical Science.

Dr. Rakshitha Jain S.B. is dedicated to promoting holistic health through Ayurveda and continues to make significant contributions to the field through her research and practice.
